Order a certified document

Use this service to order a certified copy of a document in a company's filing history.

You'll need:

  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account
  • a debit or credit card

Orders for incorporation documents consist of only the memorandum and articles. If these are not available, we will include IN01 or an equivalent documents instead.

{{ govukInsetText({ text: "You cannot use this service to order a copy of the certificate of incorporation that was issued when the company was formed. It can only be ordered by calling Companies House on 0303 1234 500." }) }}

If you need a certificate that confirms company details and can include a statement of good standing (also known as a certificate of good standing), you will need to use our Order a certificate service.

Ordering a certified document takes around 5 minutes.

{{ govukButton({ text: "Start now", classes: "govuk-!-margin-top-2 govuk-!-margin-bottom-8", isStartButton: true }) }}

Fee

It costs £15 for a certified document with standard delivery. Incorporation orders cost £30, and include two documents (the memorandum and articles).

The order can be sent to a UK or international address. We will aim to dispatch the order within 4 working days.

You cannot pay using a Companies House account.
