Order a certified document

Use this service to order certified copies of documents in a company's filing history.

For now, you can only order a certified copy of a document if we have it in a digital format in the company's filing history.

To check if the documents you need are available in a digital format, search for the company in the Companies House service and select the filing history tab.

You can order more than one certified document using this service.

Ordering certified documents takes around 5 minutes.

Fee

It costs:

  • £15 for each certified document
  • £30 for new incorporation documents (including the memorandum and articles)

The order will be sent by standard delivery to a UK or international address. We will dispatch the order within 4 working days.

You can pay by debit or credit card. You cannot pay using a Companies House account.
