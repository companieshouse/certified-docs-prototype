Order a certified document

Use this service to order certified copies of digital documents from a company's filing history for delivery by post.

To check available documents:

  • search for the company in the Companies House service
  • select the company's filing history to view the list of digital documents available

You can order more than one certified document using this service.

Ordering certified documents takes around 5 minutes.

Fee

Certified documents cost £15 each. New incorporation documents cost £30 and include the memorandum and articles.

The order is sent by standard delivery to a UK or international address. We will dispatch the order within 4 working days.

You can pay by debit or credit card. You cannot pay using a Companies House account.
